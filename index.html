Our Space

Access to our venue and full board game collection is $5/person, with a 15% discount for groups of 6+ people. Come by, find a table, and stay for as long as you’d like.

Not looking to play games? That’s okay too, we make a great study & work space with our tables and wifi.

The venue fee ($5) also grants you a complimentary hour of PC/console access for the same day (expires at closing).

PCs and tables can be reserved ahead of time for a $5 fee, which is only refundable with more than 24hr notice.

Digital Entertainment

Purchase PC time for $5/hour — these hours stay on your account so you can come back any time to keep playing. You can also purchase a day pass for $15.

Our Tier 1 PCs have AMD Ryzen 5 5600X processors, GeForce RTX 4070s, and 144Hz 2K monitors.

We also have a set of Tier 2 PCs with GTX 780s and 1080p monitors that cost less to use, a great option if you don’t need the extra power or just want to browse the web.

Food & Drink

Our food menufood menu is available from 11am–9pm, while snacks and drinks are available at all hours. Beer & wine is served from 4pm to close.

No outside food or drink is allowed except water.
