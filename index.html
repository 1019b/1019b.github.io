Welcome to kin! We are a local, family-run business offering a casual space to work, study, and enjoy board games and video games. Our multi-purpose building includes a homestyle restaurant, as well as an eclectic collection of antiques, vintage jewelry, and gifts for sale. We hope you’ll come by and check us out!

Board Games & Workspace

For $5 / person you can access our board game collection, as well as use our wifi & power outlets to study or work; find a table and stay as long as you like!

Digital Entertainment

PC access is $5 / hour, and prepaid hours stay on your account so you can come back any time to keep playing. We also offer a day pass for $20.

Food & Drink

We serve food from 12pm to 8pm, and beer & wine from 4pm to close. Please don’t bring any outside food or drink except water, thank you.
